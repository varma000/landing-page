FAQ

Questions marketers ask

Do I need coding skills? +
No. This is a no-code course. You'll use AI tools through their normal interfaces — if you can write a brief, you can do this.
Will I get a real campaign in my portfolio? +
Yes. By week 5 you'll have a full multi-channel campaign — personas, copy, creatives, calendar, and a live dashboard — ready to show clients or employers.
Is this useful for freelancers and agencies? +
Absolutely. Freelancers use it to take more clients; agencies use it to deliver more campaigns per team without adding headcount.
How is this different from the Beginners course? +
Beginners is a gentle first step for non-tech learners. This course is outcome-driven and assumes basic marketing awareness — built to ship real campaigns, not to explain what AI is.
