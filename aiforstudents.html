FAQ

Questions parents ask

Is this safe for my child? +
Yes. Every session is teacher-supervised, the ethics module is mandatory, batches are small, and we require parental consent. No unsupervised browsing, ever.
Does my child need a laptop? +
A basic laptop or desktop helps for the website-building weeks, but we can guide you on minimum requirements during the free demo class. Tablets work for most sessions.
Is there a certificate? +
Yes — your child receives a Certificate of Completion after the showcase and capstone project.
What if my child has zero tech background? +
Perfect — that's exactly who this is for. No experience needed. We start from "What is AI?" using fun, everyday analogies.
